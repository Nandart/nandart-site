About NANdART

NANdART is a gallery where art walks with feet in the present and eyes fixed on the infinite. Created by a Santomean artist, it blends tradition and vanguard to welcome creators seeking to eternalize their works in the digital thread of time. Each piece is carefully received and reborn on the blockchain as an NFT — not merely as a product, but as a vessel of soul and memory.

Are you an artist? Join the new age of art.

NANdART invites visual, digital, plastic, or experimental artists to showcase their works in the Web3 world. Here, each sale is yours. Every resale too.

Web3 and Art

Web3 represents a new era for digital art. At NANdART, we explore the potential of NFTs to ensure ownership, rarity, and continuous value to artistic creations.
