Terms & Responsibilities

Welcome to NANdART. These terms define the legal framework, rights, obligations, and revenue shares between artists and the gallery. By submitting artwork, each artist agrees to the following conditions:

Ownership Primary Sales Secondary Sales Artist Responsibilities Gallery Responsibilities NFT Rights Disclaimer

1. Ownership and Copyright Artist retains full rights

All submitted artworks remain the exclusive intellectual property of the artist. NANdART receives a non-exclusive right to display, mint and sell the work as an NFT on behalf of the artist. This right is revocable until minting or sale.

2. Primary Sales First sale distribution model

  • 90% of proceeds go directly to the artist’s wallet.
  • 10% is retained by NANdART to support operations and infrastructure.

3. Secondary Sales Royalties from resales

  • 6% of each resale goes to the original artist.
  • 4% is allocated to NANdART for platform sustainability and visibility.

4. Artist Responsibilities Ethical and legal obligations

  • Guarantee that the submitted work is original and fully authored by the artist.
  • Ensure the content does not infringe third-party rights.
  • Accept curatorial decisions without appeal.
  • Indicate whether the Premium Service is selected for central gallery display.

5. Gallery Responsibilities What NANdART guarantees

  • Provide fair and independent curatorial review of all submissions.
  • Display accepted works either in the main gallery (premium) or on the Web3 & NFT page (standard).
  • Ensure secure minting, payment handling and metadata integrity.
  • Uphold the artist’s copyright and never alter or reproduce the work without written consent.

6. NFT Rights Clarifying digital ownership

  • The NFT buyer receives a unique, verifiable token of authenticity.
  • Copyright and reproduction rights remain with the original artist.
  • No physical or commercial rights are granted unless explicitly stated.

7. Disclaimer & Termination Conditions of removal and closure

NANdART reserves the right to remove any submission or minted NFT in the event of proven fraud, plagiarism, or breach of terms. The artist may also request removal at any time prior to minting or sale.

By continuing to use this platform, the artist affirms their understanding and agreement with these terms, and accepts NANdART’s commitment to transparency, integrity, and the elevation of digital art.
